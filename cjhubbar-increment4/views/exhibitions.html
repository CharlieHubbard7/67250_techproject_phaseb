Echoes in the Static

Step into a room where sound becomes visible. Echoes in the Static is an immersive audio-visual installation that transforms ambient noise — city sounds, human voices, natural frequencies — into cascading waves of light and color. No two visits are ever the same. The exhibition responds to you, and to everyone around you.

The Unfinished Canvas

What does it mean for a piece of art to be "done"? The Unfinished Canvas is a collaborative, ever-evolving exhibition where visitors contribute brushstrokes, words, and digital marks to a shared living artwork. Revisit it a week later and find it completely transformed — shaped by hundreds of hands you'll never meet.

Ghost in the Grid

Part photography, part data visualization, Ghost in the Grid examines the digital footprints we leave behind without realizing it. Using anonymized public data, this exhibition renders the invisible patterns of modern life into striking, large-scale visual art — and asks: if your data were a painting, what would it look like?
